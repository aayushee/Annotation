Dr Rainsford on New York landlord
BOSTON Nov 15 In the Episcopal Congress
today the Rev Dr William S Rainsford of
New York said that unskilled labor had scarcely
a chance under the conditions of our large
cities The poor suffered in New York from the
exaction of their landlords
They must help the poor to fight their land
lords who were simply ruthless bloodsuckers
He believed in the educating influence of the
trade union
