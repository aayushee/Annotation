Who Wants a Job Lot of Trolley Cars
A levy was made by the Sheriff of Essex
county yesterday upon fifty trolley cars of the
Consolidated Traction Company In Newark to
satisfy a verdict of $7000 damages to John
Kelly for injuries received Leonard Kalish
his counsel obtained a verdict of $12000 at the 
time of the trial but the Supreme Court cut It
down to $7000 The running of the cars was
not stopped but the numbers will I betaken
down and they will be advertised for sale on
Nov 22
