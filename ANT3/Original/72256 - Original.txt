A Heidelberg medical Graduate Killed by 
a Fall
Adam Schnorr  a German  47 years old  of 51 
Avenue A  was killed by falling down  stairs at 
his boarding house early yesterday morning
He came to his country a few months ago  after 
having been graduated in medicine at the Hei
delberg University Being unable to speak Eng
lish  he had not tried to practise his profession 
in this city. He supported himself by selling 
wines and liquors for a New York firm He 
was intemperate  and it is supposed that his fall 
was due to his being drunk
