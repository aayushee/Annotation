DREXEL MORGAN & CO
The Firm Name Will Be Changed to J P
Morgan & Co on Jan 1 1895
It is understood that when the partnership of
Drexel Morgan & Co expires by limitation on
Jan 1 1895 the firm will be reorganized to con
tinue business under the title of J P Morgan &
Co The title of the London and Philadelphia
houses will remain unchanged and the Paris 
house will be Morgan Harjes & Co These
changes had been decided on before the death of
Mr J Hood Wright
