Col james W knapp dying
Warsaw nov 15  Col james W knapp one 
of gen sheridans trusted officers during the
war of rebellion, lies at the point of death.
He was striken with paralysis on Monday 
last and since has been unconscious. He re
cruited historic D company of new 
york dragoons of which company he was made
captain in august 1862 he was elected major
of regiment in december 1864 and suc
ceeded to the command He led the regiment 
in fortyfour battles In politics he is a demo
crat and was postmaster of this village from 
1852 to 1861 Although the town was opposed
to him politically he was elected justice of the
peace which office he held continously for 
over forty years His death is only a question
of hours
