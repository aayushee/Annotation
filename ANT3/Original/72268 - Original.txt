Raided a Park Avenue Poker Game
For some time the policemen on the post on
Park avenue near the Grand Central station
have been reporting to acting Capt Robb that 
gambling was carried on in the saloon kept by 
Julius Schroeder in the basement of 124 Park 
avenue Capt Robb found that the place was 
frequented by employees of the Wagner Car 
Company He raided the place last night and 
found Schroeder and three others playing poker
Ex Senator Cullen gave bail for the lot
