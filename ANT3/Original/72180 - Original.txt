Our Salvation Army Congressman
From the Chicago Record
Cy Sulloway one of the newly elected
members of the house from New Hampshire is 
a Captain in the Salvation Army and married a
salvation lassie In Boston a few years ago He
is 6 feet 6 inches tall in his socks and is said to
be an earnest and fluent talker During his short
life he has played many parts having been a
preacher a lawyer and a doctor In politics he
was first a Democrat then a Greenbacker and
now a Republican
