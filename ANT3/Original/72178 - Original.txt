Six Widows Testified Against Them

recent sextuple lynching the six widows of the
murdered men testified in succession They
told the circumstances of the arrest of their
husbands by Detective Richardson and said
that he might have taken the prisoners to Mem
phis by train instead of In a frail wagon and
over a rough road at night
